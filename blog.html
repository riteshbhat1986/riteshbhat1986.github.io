July 2025 – Integrating AI into Engineering Curricula

The future of engineering education depends on how seamlessly we blend core principles with emerging technologies. AI and machine learning are no longer standalone subjects—they must be meaningfully embedded across disciplines to enhance relevance and employability.

June 2025 – Reflections on Outcome-Based Education (OBE)

Implementing OBE has helped streamline assessment and course delivery. However, sustained faculty development, industry alignment, and contextual adaptation remain critical for its success within the Indian higher education ecosystem.

May 2025 – Project-Based Learning in Mechatronics
