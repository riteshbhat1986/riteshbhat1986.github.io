About Me

I currently serve as Professor and Head of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai. My academic pursuits focus on industrial systems, composite materials, intelligent automation, and engineering education. I am a published author, academic editor, and research mentor committed to outcome-based education and interdisciplinary development.

  • Associate Editor – Discover Applied Sciences (Springer Nature)
  • Editor-in-Chief – Journal of Computers, Mechanical and Management
  • Guest Editor – MDPI Engineering Proceedings
  • Lead Editor – Springer Civil Infrastructures Series & Proceedings of Engineering Sciences

International Academic Experience

  • Manipal University Dubai – Mechanical Engineering Lecturer
