Profile Summary

Dr. Ritesh Ramakrishna Bhat currently serves as Professor and Head of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai. With over 15 years of academic and research experience, he has held faculty positions in India, Malaysia, and Dubai. His contributions span curriculum development, international collaborations, student mentoring, and institutional strategy.
