About Me

I serve as Professor and Head of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai. I am also the Deputy Strategic Project Officer, supporting institutional growth through collaborative R&D and strategic initiatives.

My academic and research interests include smart manufacturing systems, embedded intelligence, cyber-physical systems, and AI applications in mechatronics.
