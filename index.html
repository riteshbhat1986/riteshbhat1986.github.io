About Me

I currently serve as Professor and Head of the Department of Mechatronics Engineering at Rajalakshmi Engineering College, Chennai. With over a decade of academic and research experience, my contributions span curriculum development, strategic partnerships, and engineering education reform. I have served in academic roles across India, Malaysia, and Dubai, and remain committed to mentoring faculty and students through a research-driven approach.
